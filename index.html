- Niklaus Wirth is best known for creating the programming languages Pascal, Euler, Algol W, and Modula.
Wirth created most of these languages in his time teaching at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology
as a professor.

Over the decades, Niklaus Wirth has written over 10 books on different topics in computer science, ranging from

and there is no doubt he has inspired many people with the work he has made.
